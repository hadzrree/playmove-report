9 · Generate Report

Copy the prompt → paste it into your preferred AI assistant (e.g. ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot or others) → paste the reply back. The prompt uses “[Child]” — no name, IC or DOB ever leaves this app.

In the real app you would paste the AI assistant's reply below; it is parsed into the sections and “[Child]” becomes the preferred name locally. You may further refine any section using your preferred AI assistant.

AI text is a draft. All clinical interpretation remains yours — review every section before continuing.

10 · Preview PDF

Exact template preview. Tick the sign-off to enable export.

If the SPM table colours are missing in the exported PDF, tick “Background graphics” in the print dialog (Chrome/Edge: More settings → Options).
